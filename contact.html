How is the V2V trajectory calculated? expand_more
LIFESAVER uses a proprietary distributed intelligence mesh, communicating between vehicles (V2V) and infrastructure (V2I) to predict and clear civilian congestion 450m ahead of the ambulance.
Protocol for signal override failure? expand_more
In the event of a Traffic Grid Bravo failure, the system automatically defaults to audible siren override and switches to visual HUD-alerts for all surrounding civilian units.
